Teams differ sharply between structural operators and reactive operators:

Architecture and Data are constrained by regional reporting and historical under-prioritization.

**Implication:**

Local technology strategy needs stronger authority mechanisms for architecture and earlier involvement for data.

GTEP dependency creates a service-quality and accountability gap.

**Implication:**

Infrastructure needs local leadership capacity instead of being managed indirectly through Delivery and Engineering relationship roles.

* Raul has strong trust from leadership and is becoming more useful as a structural diagnostic partner, but still needs local credibility at scale.
